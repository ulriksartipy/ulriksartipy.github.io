About

Ulrik Sartipy is a cardiac surgeon at the Heart and Vascular Theme, Karolinska University Hospital and an associate professor (docent) at the Department of Molecular Medicine and Surgery, Karolinska Institutet, Stockholm, SWEDEN.
His research interests include heart disease and clinical epidemiology. He leads a group of clinical researchers who performs nationwide population-based register studies.

The purpose of the research is to improve clinical outcomes in patients who undergoes cardiac surgery.
